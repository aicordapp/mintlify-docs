title: FAQ

---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I'm out of tokens. What do I do?">
    Don't worry — you can get more tokens for free. Use `/token claim` and vote for the bot daily to earn **70 tokens each time** you vote. There's no cost involved, either in money or additional token usage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I need help from the staff. What should I do?">
    The AICord team is happy to help you with any issues. The easiest way to get support is by opening a ticket in the support server:

    1. Join the [AICord Discord server](https://discord.aicordapp.com/).
    2. Head to the **🎫・support-tickets** channel.
    3. Follow the instructions to create a ticket.

    <Tip>
      For minor questions or quick help, you can ping **@AICord Staff** directly in the server instead of opening a full ticket.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="A feature is not working for me.">
    AICord has grown quickly and, with a small development team, some bugs may still slip through. If something isn't working as expected, please report it in the [support server](https://discord.aicordapp.com/) and the team will address it as soon as possible.

    <Note>
      The more detail you include in your report — such as the command used, what happened, and what you expected — the faster the team can investigate and fix the issue.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Someone is impersonating me with a character. How do I stop them?">
    Using AI characters to impersonate or defame real people is a violation of the [AICord Terms of Service](/legal/terms-of-service). If this happens to you, report the impersonator in the [support server](https://discord.aicordapp.com/) and the team will take action, which may include banning the server or removing the AI character.

    <Warning>
      Do not attempt to retaliate or resolve the situation yourself. Report it directly to the AICord team so the appropriate action can be taken.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
