## How AICord works

AICord lives in your Discord server as a bot. Once invited, you can use slash commands or the [dashboard](https://dashboard.aicordapp.com) to:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invite AICord to your server">
    Add AICord to your Discord server from [aicordapp.com](https://aicordapp.com). You'll need **Manage Server** permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an AI character">
    Use `/character create` in any channel, or head to the [dashboard](https://dashboard.aicordapp.com) for a richer editing experience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Chat with your character">
    @mention your character's name or reply to its messages to start chatting. Premium users can also start a full session with `/character startsession`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore more features">
    Generate images with `/imagine`, summarize webpages, ask questions about documents, and more.
  </Step>
</Steps>
