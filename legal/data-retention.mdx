---
title: "Data Retention"
description: "How long AICord stores your data"
---

<Note>If AICord is kicked from your server, your data is soft deleted and scheduled for permanent erasure after **182 days**. You can restore it by reinviting AICord within that window.</Note>

When AICord is removed (kicked) from a Discord server, the associated data is **soft deleted** — it is marked as inactive and scheduled for full deletion 182 days from the date of removal.

If you reinvite AICord within those 182 days, your data is restored and the deletion schedule is cancelled.

After the 182-day period expires, all data associated with that server is **permanently erased** from AICord's systems and cannot be recovered.

This process helps AICord free up storage space for active users while giving server admins a reasonable window to change their minds.

<Info>**For server admins:** If you plan to re-add AICord after removing it, do so within 182 days to keep your characters, settings, and conversation history intact. After that point, you will need to start fresh.</Info>
